What We Bring to the
Partnership

We combine frictionless borrower onboarding with automated credit scoring to give our partners a seamless, low-risk engine for SME lending.

Origination at Scale

9+ years of SME lending. 500,000+ vetted MSMEs in our ecosystem, and $2M+ revolving disbursement per month. Banks tap our value chains without building their own origination infrastructure.

Alternative Scoring
